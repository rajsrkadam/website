Creative Direction

Automotive

A powerful brand x artist collaboration between Raftaar and Harley, this campaign brings together hip-hop culture, motorcycle lifestyle, and high-performance attitude. Raftaar’s strong youth connect and street credibility make him the perfect cultural bridge for a brand built around freedom, rebellion, and individuality. The campaign is designed to travel beyond traditional advertising by tapping into music audiences, biking communities, lifestyle followers, and digital-first fans. With bold visuals, sharp styling, and high-energy storytelling, the collaboration creates strong social recall across reels, performance edits, launch content, and community-led conversations. Raftaar x Harley positions the brand not just as a motorcycle label, but as a symbol of ambition, movement, and self-expression.
